AI Recruiter technology

At the center of our most powerful sourcing technologies is AI Recruiter. It leverages our knowledge from hosting 160m candidates in 1.5 million jobs to make your next hire easier. A brainchild of our data science team, it’s been in development since 2017 - and it’s only getting better. AI Recruiter harnesses the power of publicly available information to bring you:

Jobcart Chrome extension

As a recruiter or hiring manager, stumbling across someone on LinkedIn or Twitter is always a bonus. Simply click the Jobcart extension in Chrome, and now you have a full profile - contact information with the best contact info, other social accounts, and work experience.

Boolean skills search

If you’re a specialized recruiter looking for a very particular set of skills, our passive candidate search puts the power of the internet in your hands. You’re likely not going to get a response from an Engineer or Developer on LinkedIn, so why not just go straight to their inbox? Aggregating publicly available information, you’ll get the richest profiles and best contact information for each passive candidate.

Attract more candidates

Careers page builder

Forget asking your technical team to build or update your careers page. With Jobcart, creating a beautiful careers page that puts your brand and jobs in the spotlight doesn’t take any code – just a few drags, drops, and clicks. With auto-fill applications and mobile optimization, it couldn’t be easier to impress and convert your next candidate.

One-click posting to 200+ job sites

Get your job in front of millions of candidates. All with a single click. When you submit a job, Jobcart automatically posts it to 200+ job sites to ensure it shows up wherever people search for work, including Google, Indeed, LinkedIn and more. As applications come in, we collect and organize them, then add them to your Jobcart pipeline. So you can review candidates from hundreds of job sites, all in one place

Easy social sharing

Your own social channels can be a key driver of new candidates. Make posting to your social channels easy - and part of every job posting process.

Get others involved

Triple your referrals and promote your own

Engaging referral platform for employees

It’s easy for employees, and easier for you. We’ll notify employees of new openings automatically, and give them unique sharing links so they can share your jobs with their networks. With their own reports, employees can track their referrals and view any rewards you’re offering.

Internal applications

Create your own internal application and automatically notify your employees about new jobs that you’d like to fill from within. Your internal jobs can now follow the same process as external jobs. They get a chance to grow. You save time and money. Everybody wins.
