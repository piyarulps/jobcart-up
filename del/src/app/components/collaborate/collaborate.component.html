Fair and consistent evaluation

Guide your hiring teams through fair and objective evaluations so you can mitigate bias and move the best candidates forward.

Anonymized screening

Anonymized screening encourages a more inclusive hiring process by mitigating unconscious bias from the start. Our native anonymized screening feature focuses on the sourced and applied stages of the hiring process. When enabled, anonymized screening hides the following from the Candidate Profile and CV:

  • Candidate name
  • Candidate address
  • Candidate phone number

Interview kits and scorecards

When you’re hiring as a team you need accessible, actionable feedback. Create an interview kit for a structured interview process and ensure that every candidate is assessed accurately and consistently:

  • All candidates are asked the same questions, in the same order
  • Responses are evaluated via a scorecard using a consistent rating scale
  • The scorecards you create are automatically attached to the appropriate stage of the candidate’s profile, accessible by the whole team
  • Feedback is gathered on the candidate profile, where the team can review and take action

Assessments

Make objective, data-driven decisions on which candidates proceed forward. Jobcart Assessments help our customers hire the right candidates fairly and impartially. With four cognitive tests and a personality test, gauge the aptitude of your candidate pool and interview the candidates best fit for the role.

  • Send assessments to candidates directly from the Jobcart platform
  • Access results accompanied by predictive job performance insights right on the candidate timeline
  • Hiring managers can compare candidate scores, leave feedback, and review insights, all in one place

Keep hiring managers in the know

Ensure your hiring team knows what’s up, what’s next and what’s left to do. At each login, Jobcart greets users with a clear view of where things stand for each job and every candidate. Need to ask a question to a teammate? You can @ mention their name for a quick reply, without leaving the system.

2-way calendar syncing

Jobcart offers a two-way sync with email accounts like Gmail and Outlook, so any action you take in Jobcart automatically appears in your own email, and vice versa. Stop manually forwarding emails and switching apps just to reply to a candidate or mark an email as read. Even better, all conversations are tracked and posted to the candidate’s timeline for the entire hiring team to reference.

Notifications and action items

Move quickly to secure the best candidates. Hiring managers can stay up to speed with inbox items, email alerts and mobile notifications. With a click, approve new requisitions or offers, review new applicants and follow up on internal conversations.

Hiring team access roles and rights

Simplify collaboration with tools that control who sees what, when, and set your hiring teams up for success from the start. User roles define which hiring tasks team members should (and can) perform, and user permissions ensure they get just the right amount of access to get the job done. Both can be set in just a few clicks.

Manage candidates

With a candidate database, automatic recordkeeping, and customizable data fields, staying organized and on track with hiring is effortless. Jobcart intuitive design means it’s easy for the entire recruiting team to get started.

Candidate database

Search, filter and interact with a complete database of your candidates, automatically built to house your applicants, passively sourced candidates, referrals and more. Look up candidates based on skills or resume details, narrow down the results with custom tagging, and contact the best candidates in bulk.

Custom fields

Track key candidate information that’s most important to your own business like start dates, requested salary and whatever other data you need. Add profile fields which candidates can complete when they apply, or the hiring team can fill out internally. Control access to profile fields so that any sensitive information is kept private.

Bulk communication to candidates

Automated actions let you communicate with applicants in bulk, on your own timeline. Streamline repetitive administrative tasks and candidate communications, set up triggers for each stage of the hiring process, schedule candidate emails (including delayed DQ), and allow candidates to automatically self-schedule meeting times to save recruiters and hiring managers time and effort.

Schedule efficiently

Spend less time booking (and rebooking) interviews. Tools for self-scheduling, multi-part interviews, event landing pages and more enable you to create an improved candidate experience and achieve better interview outcomes.

Self-scheduling

Connect your team’s calendars to empower candidates to self-schedule interviews. No unnecessary back and forth when it comes to choosing a time - the candidate can see available time slots on the interviewer’s schedule and select one that works. And if plans change, you or the candidate can reschedule in seconds.

Landing page
